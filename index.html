About.

I am a 4th-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at National University of Singapore. I am advised by Prof. Yang You.

My research interests span the fields of machine learning system, especially in parallel systems for large models. My primary objective is to build high performance systems for large-scale machine learning. Currently, I would like to spend my time on two exciting topics: automated parallelization and communication optimization , and high-performance training system based on the ARM platform.
